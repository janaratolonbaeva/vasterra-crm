Why customize a CRM system if everything works smoothly already?

Off-the-shelf CRM is a universal solution that suits almost any business but does not completely cover the needs of any of them. This also applies to brokers. As a result, many tasks have to be done manually which requires more time and effort, which ultimately affects the company's overall performance.

Customizing CRM or developing a system from scratchhelps businesses with the following:

Strengthening client relationships and increasing profits from each of them

Saving hundreds of thousands of dollars on subscription fees for off-the-shelf solutions

More effectively managing a larger number of clients

Boosting revenue in the brokerage sector

Sharing information among departments to enhance collaboration

Saving brokers' time on tasks they shouldn't do

How is the workflow for CRM automation built

The project consists of four key stages. At each of them, you will only need to maintain minimal involvement: you won't have to be distracted from your main body of work, and most importantly, you won't lose any clients. After implementation, you will definitely see an increase in their number.

Data collection

Business analysts and project managers conduct interviews with representatives from all departments, defining your company's goals and development requirements.

Data analysis and project approval

Based on the collected information, we develop an understanding of the task, create an efficient work schedule, and calculate the budget. We approve all documentation with the client.

Development of new modules

We work iteratively, presenting results to stakeholders at each stage. We launch the system and gather feedback for further improvements.

Team training and system maintenance

We prepare training materials and video tutorials, maintain the system's functionality after the launch, and if necessary, implement other new features.
