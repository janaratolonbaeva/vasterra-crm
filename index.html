Why customize a CRM system if everything works smoothly already?

Off-the-shelf CRM is a universal solution that suits almost any business but does not completely cover the needs of any of them. This also applies to brokers. As a result, many tasks have to be done manually which requires more time and effort, which ultimately affects the company's overall performance.

Customizing CRM or developing a system from scratchhelps businesses with the following:

Strengthening client relationships and increasing profits from each of them

Saving hundreds of thousands of dollars on subscription fees for off-the-shelf solutions

More effectively managing a larger number of clients

Boosting revenue in the brokerage sector

Sharing information among departments to enhance collaboration

Saving brokers' time on tasks they shouldn't do

Which modules
could be useful for you

What it can do

Customization changes the logic of how the base modules operate. Mobile adaptation is necessary for the system to function correctly on any device, allowing brokers to work in the CRM even on smartphones.

When it can be useful

If you need to create a presentation for a client directly from your smartphone, on the go and from anywhere.

What it can do

It can create floor plans for each property, making it easier to maintain the offices and keep them up-to-date. A special dashboard shows which properties require the relevant updates.

When it can be useful

When you need to view historical data about a property, understand the speed of its updates, and assess demand for specific areas. By examining who occupies the business center and when their contracts expire, you can re-engage clients in a timely manner.

What it can do

It shows the clients’ dynamics from different sectors. For example, it may indicate that in the agricultural sector, both employee numbers and revenue are increasing.

When it can be useful

If you need to analyze market trends to make strategic decisions based on the data obtained.

What it can do

If suitable options matching the client's request appear within the CRM, the system immediately notifies the broker. With just a few clicks, they can then create a presentation or online page and then share the link with the client.

When it can be useful

If there's a need to quickly provide the client with up-to-date information about a property— including photos of offices, floor plans, and rate analysis.

What it can do

It can sort properties based on the precise requirements of the client. You can highlight a specific area on the map and receive a list of properties located there.

When it can be useful

If you need to find premises with specific parameters, such as those not on the ground floor or only with an open layout.

What it can do

It shows which properties received the most inquiries, and which ones had the highest conversion rates from inquiry to viewing, and from viewing to contract.

When it can be useful

If you need to assess which customer acquisition channel has been more effective and how to further allocate the advertising budget.

What it can do

It can display various segments and help build reports. For example, on the dashboards, you can see the percentage of a premises` vacancy depending on the area, dynamics of demand for different spaces, trends in rental rate changes, distribution of demand by districts, etc.

When it can be useful

If you need to create a custom report for the brokerage department and save time on manual data collection and updates.

What it can do

Automatically collects and updates EBID data from departments in real-time, as well as sales funnels, showing the stage of all deals.

When it can be useful

If you need to review forecasts for deal closures and projected revenue to understand how current metrics compare with planned ones across departments and with specific individuals.

What it can do

Shows the percentage of the market occupied by the broker compared to other players and identifies which specific deals and marketing activities contributed to this difference.

When it can be useful

If it's necessary to understand how market conditions are impacting company metrics: why revenue is declining, which sectors of the economy are growing, etc.

How is the workflow for CRM automation built

The project consists of four key stages. At each of them, you will only need to maintain minimal involvement: you won't have to be distracted from your main body of work, and most importantly, you won't lose any clients. After implementation, you will definitely see an increase in their number.

Data collection

Business analysts and project managers conduct interviews with representatives from all departments, defining your company's goals and development requirements.

Data analysis and project approval

Based on the collected information, we develop an understanding of the task, create an efficient work schedule, and calculate the budget. We approve all documentation with the client.

Development of new modules

We work iteratively, presenting results to stakeholders at each stage. We launch the system and gather feedback for further improvements.

Team training and system maintenance

We prepare training materials and video tutorials, maintain the system's functionality after the launch, and if necessary, implement other new features.
